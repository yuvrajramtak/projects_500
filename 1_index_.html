How FITPASS Works?

With FITPASS, fitness businesses simply list their available class slots on the platform. Our user-friendly app and website then enable customers to easily discover and book these sessions at partner studios across India.

Once a booking is made, that time slot gets blocked off exclusively for the customer who reserved it. The studio receives the booking details immediately through the FITPASS Partner Portal. After the session, the studio marks the booking as completed and gets paid seamlessly by FITPASS.

Selecting the Perfect Gym in Pune

Factors to consider when selecting a gym

Location and accessibility

Ensure that your gym or fitness centre is near your home or workplace. Exercising is an intense task, and you don’t want to drain your energy travelling or risk missing out on your workout session

Facilities and equipment

The gym you choose must be equipped with the latest workout machines and facilitate different workouts. A good gym should also have essential facilities like centralised air-conditioning, good parking space, a water cooler etc.

Trainers' expertise and qualification

The trainers at your gym should have a good amount of experience in the exercise industry and must also have relevant certifications.

Membership plans and pricing

Get detailed information on the membership plans and pricing of the gym. Enquire about what services are included or excluded from the plan.

Additional services and amenities

Many gyms and fitness centres offer add-on services or amenities like spas, body massages, locker room access, etc. Ask about available extra services and their charges.
